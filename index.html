About

Hunter. A simple, easy, and short RPG game. There's none objective in this game. All you need to do is hunt the dungeon and buy more good equipment. There is a boss in the dungeon you can make that your objective if you want. The dungeon is multi dimensional dungeon that can change its room automaticaly so you need some potion, die or deffeating the boss to escape from the dungeon. This game probably can't save your progress so make sure to don't close this tab or reload it until you finish this game.
